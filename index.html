20 years of reimagining for citizens and sustaining change

Technology is powerful, but it is only one piece of the puzzle. When it comes to enhancing the quality of life for every citizen, public systems need impact at scale, collective energy and local networks to solve local problems. Our partners and networks bring this collective energy to life. The ecosystem already exists; our task is to facilitate and amplify their efforts for 20 years, so the long-running small steps become lasting, meaningful change.
